About

I find the manipulation of space and form through color and texture fascinating. The subjects of my work are generally tied to both the surroundings in which I make the work but also the space in which the piece will stay and who might look at it.

The relation between Painting and Space is a recurring focus in my practice. Materially the work takes various forms; painting, sculpture installation often in between two and not far from the realm of construction.
